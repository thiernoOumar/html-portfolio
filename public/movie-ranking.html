The Best Movies According to Thierno

My top 3 movies of all-time

The lord of the rings

A classic fantasy film that set the standard for epic storytelling and world-building.

The Shawshank Redemption

A powerful tale of hope and friendship set against the backdrop of a brutal prison.

Forrest Gump

A heartwarming story that follows the life of a simple man with a low IQ who unwittingly influences historical events.
